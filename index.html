Australia's Tourism Boom

Australia looks busy again, but is it true? Follow along as I present the relevant data to support this claim!

When people say tourism is “back”, what is the benchmark? The amount of people coming? The amount of money flowing? Or the number of flights landing?

So, is tourism really back?

The ending is mixed: some parts of tourism are near normal, some are above old levels, and some are still behind. The final idioms make that unevenness obvious.

Which source countries recovered the most?
